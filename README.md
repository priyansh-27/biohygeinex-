# BioHygeineX

Turning responsible waste disposal into a rewarding habit.

## Overview

BioHygineX is a digital platform that helps users dispose of sanitary waste and medicines safely while promoting environmental sustainability through awareness, tracking, and reward-based engagement.

The platform combines waste collection tracking, QR-based verification, medicine management, educational resources, and a reward system into a single user-friendly solution.

## Why BioHygineX?

Millions of sanitary products and expired medicines are discarded improperly every year, leading to environmental pollution and public health risks.

BioHygineX encourages responsible disposal by making the process simple, trackable, and rewarding.

## Core Modules

### Waste Collection Tracking
Track collection vehicles serving your area and receive estimated arrival updates.

### Smart Disposal
Scan QR codes before disposal and ensure waste reaches the correct collection channel.

### Medicine Management
Monitor medicine expiry dates and support safe disposal or exchange of unused medicines.

### Rewards Program
Earn points for environmentally responsible actions and redeem them for benefits.

### Awareness Hub
Access educational content related to hygiene, waste management, and sustainability.

### Analytics
Visualize disposal activities, environmental contributions, and participation metrics.
