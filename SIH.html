Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points for waste disposal?

To earn points, scan the QR code on your sanitary product packaging before disposal. Then dispose of the product in designated sanitary waste bins. You'll automatically receive points in your account within a few minutes.

Where can I find sanitary waste bins?

Sanitary waste bins are located in schools, colleges, hospitals, shopping malls, and public spaces. Use the truck tracking feature to find collection points in your area.

How can I redeem my reward points?

Go to the Rewards section, browse available rewards, and click 'Redeem' on any reward you can afford. You'll receive a voucher code that can be used at partner stores.

What types of medicine can be exchanged?

You can exchange unexpired prescription and over-the-counter medicines. The medicine must be in original packaging and not expired. Check with partner pharmacies for specific guidelines.

How accurate is the truck tracking?

Our truck tracking uses GPS technology and is updated in real-time. The ETA provided is accurate within 5-10 minutes depending on traffic conditions.

Can I use the app without creating an account?

While you can browse educational content without an account, you need to be logged in to earn points, track disposals, redeem rewards, and access personalized features.
